About Me

I am a lawyer with over 10 years of experience in various fields of law. I have successfully represented clients in civil, criminal, family, and corporate cases. I am passionate about providing quality legal services and ensuring justice for my clients.

I graduated from the University of Nairobi with a Bachelor of Laws degree and a Master of Laws degree. I am a member of the Law Society of Kenya and the International Bar Association. I have also participated in several seminars and workshops to enhance my skills and knowledge.

My Services

Civil Law

I can help you with matters related to contracts, torts, property, inheritance, and human rights. I can assist you in drafting, reviewing, and negotiating agreements, as well as filing and defending lawsuits.

Criminal Law

I can help you with matters related to offences, penalties, and procedures. I can represent you in court, advise you on your rights and obligations, and prepare your defense strategy.

Family Law

I can help you with matters related to marriage, divorce, custody, maintenance, adoption, and succession. I can guide you through the legal process, protect your interests, and resolve your disputes.

Corporate Law

I can help you with matters related to business formation, governance, compliance, and transactions. I can advise you on the best legal structure, draft and review documents, and facilitate deals.
